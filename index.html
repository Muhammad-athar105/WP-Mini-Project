Our primary objective is to explore the positive image of Skardu Baltistan all around the world. Skardu TV is a web news channel run by a team of working journalists from Gilgit-Baltistan. This website specifically publishes or uploads important gilgit-baltistan news, while news from Pakistan in general and around the world is published or uploaded. Skardu TV also keeps broadcasting interviews of scholars from different fields for social development.
